5

The AI judge reads it — then settle

A regex can read a price; it cannot read a report. The fixed, public Qwen3-1.7B judge reads your proven report and decides YES/NO — live, on CPU, bit-for-bit deterministic. That verdict is what you commit on-chain as the claim; the committed rule settles it — no vote. Then redeem 1:1.

Same question, a different launch: the judge reads the New Glenn report — “the explosion … out of service for a year or more” — and returns NO. The word “orbit” never appears; a keyword regex couldn't call it. The judge reads meaning.

You just ran the whole loop — create, trade, prove, let the judge read it, settle — every step signed by you, on-chain.
