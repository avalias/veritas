ACT II

A judge that
reads in the open

One fixed, public AI judge reads the proven fact and returns a verdict — live, and bit-for-bit deterministic. Same evidence in, same verdict out, on any machine. It reads; a committed rule decides.

live

Type anything — it answers YES / NO / UNKNOWN with a one-line reason. A wrong computation is provable and punishable (Act III).

ACT III

A liar, slashed —
by anyone

A resolver bonded a false verdict. Anyone can dispute it: the disagreement is bisected from millions of arithmetic steps down to exactly one, which a ~330-line Sui contract re-runs itself. The liar's bond is taken — automatically, on-chain.
