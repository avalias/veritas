One market, the whole way through

A single real market on Sui, walked from trading to evidence to resolution to redemption. Every act below is a transaction you sign yourself; each toast links to it on suiscan. The state is read back from chain after each step, so what you see is what is there.

Reading the market from chain…

The outcome is a function of zkTLS-proven sources and a deterministic AI, both checked on-chain. No amount of money changes a secp256k1 verification or a fraud proof. That is the difference from a token-vote oracle: here you cannot buy the result, you can only prove it.
