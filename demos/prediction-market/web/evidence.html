Prove a fact with zkTLS, or stay out

Evidence only enters this market as a real zkTLS web proof: a signed record that a named source actually served a page saying so. The contract checks the attestor's signature with native ecrecover. There is no field for an opinion, and you cannot forge what a source said.

The sources this market trusts

Each row is one independent attestor the market pins. A proof from that source is admitted only after ecrecover recovers the attestor's address on-chain and it matches. A green row means the proof is already in the market.

Now try to cheat

Two ways people try to game an oracle. Here is what happens to each.

1. Submit an opinion. Type a verdict and submit it.

2. Forge a proof. Here is a real proof from …. Its signature recovers the pinned attestor, so the chain admits it. Now change what the source "said" and recover the signer again — this is the exact ecrecover the contract runs.

Why money can't move it

This is the difference from a token-vote oracle like UMA, where the wallet that stakes the most decides the outcome after the fact. Here the outcome is a secp256k1 signature check. No balance changes what ecrecover returns, and copies don't add up: ten copies of the BBC proof are still one trust group. To move the count you need a different real source to have reported it. You can prove a fact, not out-vote one.
