Prove a fact with zkTLS, or stay out

Evidence only enters this market as a real zkTLS web proof: a signed record that a named source actually served a page saying so. The contract checks the attestor's signature with native ecrecover. There is no field for an opinion.

The three sources this market trusts

Each row is one independent attestor the market pins. A proof from that source is admitted only after ecrecover recovers the attestor's address on-chain and it matches. A green row means the proof is already in the market.

Why copies don't add up

Each source is a separate trust group, keyed by its attestor. Submitting the BBC proof ten times still counts as one group: the market counts distinct groups that confirmed, not the number of proofs. To move the count you need a different source to have reported it, and that source's attestor has to sign a proof that ecrecover accepts. That is why you can prove a fact but cannot out-shout one.
